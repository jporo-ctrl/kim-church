The Vision

This is where vision
becomes building

The Builders Gathering is not a church service. It's a gathering of the people God is calling to build something — to be present at the beginning, to shape the culture, to carry the mission forward with their hands, their gifts, and their faith.

On July 25, Apostle Joshua Poro will walk through the culture, structure, mission, discipleship pathway, and opportunities to serve as a founding member of Kingdom Insights Ministries. You won't just hear the vision — you'll understand the part you were made to play in it.

This is the night for builders. If you're reading this, you're not here by accident.
