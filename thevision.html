The Foundation

This is a school
of the Word.

Every teaching that comes from this ministry is intended to address very specific areas of our lives. A lecturer does not come to impress you. A lecturer comes to impart a body of knowledge that transforms you. Likewise, this is a school of the Word — and the responsibility of this altar is to teach, furnish, and equip you.

There are seven levels of teaching that flow from this house. Each one is deliberate. Each one builds on the last. Together, they form the complete picture of what God is doing in and through the people of Kingdom Insights Ministries.

✦

Seven Teachings

What comes from
this altar

01

The Message of Salvation

Salvation is more than an altar call. This teaching brings the unsaved into the Kingdom and gives every believer deep intelligence concerning what actually happened to them at salvation — the forgiveness of sins, righteousness through faith, and eternal life. By the time you have been part of this house for a year, you should be able to articulate, defend, and administer the message of salvation to another person.

"Every man comes to Jesus through the convicting power of the Spirit of God. By confessing His Lordship, you receive the gifts that accompany salvation."

Acts 2:38
02

The Message of Transformation

Being saved does not automatically manifest the riches of the life you have received. The supernatural life of God is knowledge-dependent — it finds expression through a renewed mind. Transformation is you and the Holy Spirit working together through the Word. Every message from this altar is a partnership with the Holy Spirit to close the gap between who you are and who God has made you to be.

"And do not be conformed to this world, but be transformed by the renewing of your mind."

Romans 12:2
03

The Message of Empowerment

A believer may be a good Christian and yet not be an effective witness. It takes more than transformation to become an ambassador of Christ. Through repeated teachings specifically designed for this purpose, you will receive the keys that enable men to have power with God — and carry that power into every room you enter.

"But you shall receive power when the Holy Spirit has come upon you; and you shall be witnesses to Me."

Acts 1:8
04

The Message of the Supernatural

We are not just called to tell the story — we are called to validate it. Signs, wonders, miracles, breakthroughs, and a life of victory are the inheritance of every believer, not just the ministers. These teachings will help you access power, understand how it works, and walk in consistent testimony.

"And with great power the apostles gave witness to the resurrection of the Lord Jesus. And great grace was upon them all."

Acts 4:33
05

The Message of Purpose

Everybody has a purpose in Christ. Everybody has a destiny. The Great Commission captures salvation for sinners, transformation for the saints, and transformation for territories. You will hear messages about values, relationships, destiny, and your specific assignment as an agent of Kingdom advancement and societal transformation.

"For we are His workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works, which God prepared beforehand that we should walk in them."

Ephesians 2:10
06

The Message of Unity & Love

There is a unique investment of the Holy Spirit upon this ministry to bring the Body of Christ into a higher state of love and unity. The strengths we carry are not the basis for competition — they are the basis for contribution. You will see this expressed in our pursuit of balance, our mending of divisions, and our commitment to walking together rather than apart.

"Behold, how good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity!"

Psalm 133:1
07

The Message of Lasting Peace & Fulfillment

God is not only interested in your spiritual life — He is interested in your finding lasting peace and fulfillment in every dimension of living. Human relations, leadership, prosperity, meaningful work, and your eternal destiny are all within the scope of what this altar addresses. The goal is a life that is not just saved, but whole.

"Peace I leave with you, My peace I give to you; not as the world gives do I give to you. Let not your heart be troubled, neither let it be afraid."

John 14:27

The Charge

You are becoming
something intentional.

It is impossible to hear any message from this altar that does not address one, several, or all of these areas in their various expressions. The messages that come from this altar are not trial and error. We are not guessing what you should hear. Vision is involved in what you are hearing.

So if someone asks you, "Why do you come to Kingdom Insights Ministries?" — you should be able to answer. Not just, "I like the pastor," but: "In my coming, I have been taught along the lines of salvation, transformation, empowerment, the supernatural, purpose, unity, and lasting peace." When you speak like that, it becomes a wise and powerful invitation.

Sit down. Grow. The altar is ready.

"As each one has received a gift, minister it to one another, as good stewards of the manifold grace of God."

1 Peter 4:10
