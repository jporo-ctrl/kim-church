What We Believe

Statement of Faith

Genesis 1:1, John 1:1-14

God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — Creator of all things and source of all life.

John 14:6, 1 Corinthians 15:3-4

Jesus Christ

Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully God and fully man. He lived sinlessly, died for our sins, and rose on the third day, providing salvation for all who believe.

Acts 1:8, John 14:26

Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit empowers believers for Godly living, equips them with spiritual gifts, and guides them in their walk with Christ.

2 Timothy 3:16-17

The Bible

The Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God — authoritative in all matters of faith and practice.

Ephesians 2:8-9

Salvation

Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone — a free gift, not based on works, but on God's love and mercy.

John 3:16, Revelation 20:15

Eternal Life

We believe in eternal life with God for all who trust in Jesus Christ, and in the reality of eternal separation from God for those who reject Him.

"For God so loved the world that He gave His only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish but have eternal life."

John 3:16
