What Happened

The vision was cast.
The people came.

On June 27, 2026, Kingdom Insights Ministries hosted its very first public gathering — and what showed up was unmistakably the hand of God. 147 people filled the room at Compass Center in Grapevine, TX. Among them were 8 pastors and believers from 5 different nations — a room that looked like the Kingdom before the first wall even went up.

There was praise. There was worship. Apostle Joshua Poro opened the Word and ministered on The Manifold Grace of God from 1 Peter 4:10 — a message about the many-sided, ever-sufficient grace that God distributes through every member of the body. People ate together, connected, and carried something home that didn't come from a program. It came from presence.

Vision Night was not the beginning of a church. It was the beginning of a family.
