What Is Vision Night

Before you build,
you must see.

Vision Night is an open invitation to anyone who has felt a stirring — a sense that God may be calling them into something. This is not a church service. It's a conversation. Apostle Joshua Poro will openly share the full vision God has placed on his heart for Kingdom Insights Ministries — who we are, where we're going, and why now.

No commitment required. Come with your questions, your curiosity, and an open heart. If what you hear resonates, there is a place for you in this house.

What to Expect

An evening of clarity.

01

The Vision

Apostle Joshua will share the God-given vision for KIM — where it started, what it's becoming, and the assignment on this house.

02

The Mission

You'll understand the specific mandate and purpose of Kingdom Insights Ministries and how it's different from anything else in the region.

03

The Community

Meet the people who are already saying yes. Vision Night is the first gathering — you'll be in the room with the people who will become your spiritual family.

04

Your Next Step

Whether you're ready to commit or still listening, we'll share the path forward — including the Builders Gathering on July 25 and Ministry Launch on August 29.
